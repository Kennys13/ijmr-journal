About The Journal

The International Journal of Multidisciplinary Research (IJMR) is a premier, peer-reviewed, and fully open-access journal dedicated to fostering and disseminating high-quality scholarly work across the entire academic spectrum.

Our Mission

To promote excellence in research across all academic disciplines and facilitate the sharing of discoveries.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic global platform for researchers, practitioners, and educators to publish innovative work.
